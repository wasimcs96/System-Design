# Development Plan – Next Year (Aligned with KPI Gaps)

---

1. Improve Sprint Planning Accuracy and Delivery Predictability

 Development Area / Goal

Enhance sprint estimation accuracy and improve delivery predictability to consistently meet sprint commitments.

 Plan of Action

* Improve story breakdown and estimation techniques during sprint planning
* Use historical sprint data (velocity, spillover) to refine estimates
* Proactively identify risks, dependencies, and blockers earlier in the sprint
* Regularly review sprint retrospectives and apply improvement actions

 Target Result / Improvement

* Achieve consistent sprint Say/Do ratio above 90%
* Reduce story spillover and unplanned work
* Improved predictability and stakeholder confidence in delivery

---

2. Strengthen Code Quality, Testing Practices, and Defect Prevention

 Development Area / Goal

Enhance code quality and reduce production defects through stronger testing discipline and proactive quality controls.

 Plan of Action

* Increase focus on test-driven development (TDD) and test coverage
* Improve quality of code reviews with emphasis on security, performance, and maintainability
* Actively monitor SonarQube reports and address high-severity issues early
* Collaborate more closely with QA to improve defect prevention before release

 Target Result / Improvement

* Reduction in critical and recurring production issues
* Improved unit and integration test coverage
* Higher overall code quality and system stability

---

3. Enhance System Architecture Ownership and Technical Decision-Making

 Development Area / Goal

Strengthen architectural decision-making and proactively manage technical debt to improve system scalability and maintainability.

 Plan of Action

* Improve quality and clarity of Technical Design Documents (TDDs)
* Seek early architectural reviews and feedback from peers and Engineering Manager
* Actively track and prioritize technical debt in Jira
* Continuously evaluate system performance, scalability, and reliability impacts

 Target Result / Improvement

* Better-aligned and well-documented technical designs
* Reduced architecture-related rework and production issues
* Improved long-term platform stability and maintainability

---

4. Develop Leadership Effectiveness Through Mentorship and Knowledge Sharing

 Development Area / Goal

Strengthen technical leadership skills by improving mentorship, communication, and knowledge sharing within the squad.

 Plan of Action

* Conduct regular technical mentoring and guidance sessions with squad members
* Increase knowledge-sharing activities such as tech talks, documentation, and pair programming
* Improve clarity and completeness of technical documentation in Confluence
* Actively support team members’ skill development and learning goals

 Target Result / Improvement

* Improved technical capability and confidence within the squad
* Better onboarding and knowledge continuity
* Stronger collaboration and team engagement

---

Why these 4 points work well for a Rating = 2

* Acknowledge room for improvement (not defensive)
* Directly linked to your 4 KPIs
* Clearly measurable and realistic
* Signals growth mindset and leadership maturity
