## 📚 Top Preparation Tips for NEOM / Tonomus

> **1. Study Smart City Architecture**
> - Learn Digital Twin concepts (city replica in software)
> - Understand IoT at scale: sensors → edge computing → cloud → AI
> - Read about smart traffic systems, smart utilities, smart buildings

> **2. Master Large-Scale Distributed Systems**
> - Design systems for 1M+ concurrent users
> - Study real-time event streaming (Kafka, Kinesis)
> - Edge computing + fog computing patterns

> **3. Know AI/ML Integration Patterns**
> - You don't need to build models — but know how to design platforms that serve ML models at scale
> - MLOps concepts, feature stores, model serving

> **4. Prepare "Moonshot" Stories from Your Career**
> - NEOM rejects conventional thinkers
> - Prepare stories where YOU proposed something bold, unconventional, or unprecedented
> - Quantify scale: "served 10M users", "reduced latency by 80%", "first system of its kind"

> **5. Research NEOM Deeply**
> - Watch: NEOM official YouTube channel
> - Read: NEOM.com/en-gb/programs/tonomus
> - Know all NEOM sub-projects: THE LINE, SINDALAH, OXAGON, TROJENA
> - Understand Vision 2030 goals and how tech enables them

> **6. "Why NEOM?" Answer is Critical**
> - They turn away people who say "for money" or "for career growth"
> - They want: "I want to build something that has never been built before"
